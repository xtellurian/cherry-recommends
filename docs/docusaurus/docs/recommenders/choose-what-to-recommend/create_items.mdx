<TabItem value="web">

First, go to `Library` in the header and select `Items`.
You now should see a catalogue of all recommendable items. 

Click `Create Recommendable Item` in the top-right.
Now fill out the details of the item.
The `Item Identifier` should be a name that works in your stack can could be a `Discount Code`, `SKU Id`, or `Plan Id`.
Add in the `Price` and your `Cost` of the `Item`.

Finally, hit Create and you're done!
