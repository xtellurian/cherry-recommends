---


Cherry Recommends increase lifetime value of customers by increasing revenue at chosen part of the customer journey.

## Time to value

You should start to see statistically significant results within 2 weeks of correctly deploying Cherry with sufficient data. 
Your revenue should consistently increase for 3 to 9 months after until it achieves an optimal result.

## Return on investment

Your monthly return on investment is the extra revenue Cherry provides (see reporting) divided by monthly cost.
